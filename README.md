## :dart: Goal
Our main goal is a better understanding of the development of Israeli music culture over the years based on a statistical analysis of the lyrics over the decades.   
&nbsp;  

## :bar_chart: Results
#### :male_sign: Gender
Here we check the gender of the artists over the decades

#### :sparkling_heart: Emotions
Here we check if the songs are sad or happy.
